Our eco-friendly journey

Sustainable Dining

At Bella Verde, sustainability is our number one priority and influences everything we do. Our farm-to-table approach ensures that we purchase fresh and seasonal ingredients from local farms, reducing transportation distances and supporting our community of hard-working farmers by allowing them to grow what works best. Every dish is prepared carefully using traditional Mediterranean and Italian culinary techniques, crafted fresh daily, repurposing scraps for broths and composting leftovers in order to reduce our waste output. Beyond the kitchen, we prioritize eco-friendly practices in other ways, from using biodegradable packaging in our orders to using highly energy-efficient appliances and robust recycling programs. By combining these values and practices, we’re proud to offer meals that are delicious, wholesome, and kind to the planet.

Farm-to-table

Our ingredients come from small, non-corporate farms that focus on sustainability. Every dish we make highlights freshness and supports local communities. We source responsibly, ensuring every ingredient meets our high standards for quality and ethical farming.

The Process

We follow Mediterranean and Italian traditions, crafting vegan pasta, slow-roasted vegetables, and fresh ingredients daily. Every dish is made in-house to order, guaranteeing a delicious, authentic experience. Our process ensures quality, freshness, and a true taste of tradition.

Green Dining

We protect the planet through sustainable practices, using compostable packaging, conserving water, and repurposing scraps into broth or compost. Every step of our process is designed to reduce waste and lower our footprint. Sustainability is at the core of everything we do.

Our Approach to Green Dining

Eliminating Single-Use Plastics

The issue: The widespread adoption of single-use plastics has contributed to increases in landfill waste and environmental pollution.

Our Solution: We have replaced single-use plastics in our kitchen with landfill-biodegradable alternatives, including cling film, gloves, and bin bags, which biodegrade 90% faster than conventional plastics and capture methane for waste-to-energy production without breaking down into microplastics. Additionally, we have transitioned to reusable containers for food storage and preparation, further reducing our use disposable plastics to be virtually nonexistent.

Eco-Friendly Cleaning with eWater

The issue: The traditional cleaning products that most establisments use rely on harsh chemicals that contribute to water pollution and increased health risks.

Our Solution: We have switched to eWater, an electrolyzed water system that replaces 80% of the conventional chemical cleaners, including degreasers, sanitizers, and floor cleaners. It’s safe, sustainable, and only requires water, salt, and electricity to produce, making it very efficient and sustainable. We also encourage our staff to bring their own spray bottles and bring our eWater home for household usage, helping to reduce household chemical waste as well, making sure our impact reachers beyond our small restaurant.

Sustainable Bottled Water Program

The issue: The process of importing bottled water leads to significant increases in carbon emissions due to transportation times and methods.

Our Solution: We now source both our still and sparkling water from a local supplier, reducing emissions, minimizing waste, and ensuring fresher, high-quality water. This shift supports our local community and furthers our strong commitment to sustainability. By reducing reliance on imported bottled water, we make sure that even something as simple as drinking water will leave a positive impact on the environment.

Our Unique Cooking Process

Sustainable cooking supports ethical farming, reduces waste, and preserves flavors while protecting our planet.

Cold-Pressed Oils

We use cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil to retain its natural antioxidants, healthy fats, and vibrant flavors.

Slow Roasting

Our vegetables are slow-roasted at low temperatures to enhance their natural sweetness and depth of flavor.

Fermentation

We ferment cashew cheese, pickled vegetables, and sourdough crust for deep flavor and gut-friendly probiotics.

Sprouting

Legumes, nuts, and grains are sprouted to improve their nutritional value and digestibility. This process increases vitamin content.
