Core values

Our Approach

At Bella Verde, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. Our commitment to sustainability, fresh preparation, and farm-to-table practices ensures that every dish we serve is both delicious and responsible. By sourcing locally, reducing waste, and prioritizing eco-friendly methods, we minimize our environmental impact while maximizing flavor. We believe that mindful cooking not only enhances our meals but also helps towards creating a healthier planet for future generations.

Backstory

Green Beginnings


From its founding in 2015, Bella Verde has its roots sunk deep into a family farm that has seen generations in the Mediterranean countryside. A place where we felt the earth was part of us, connected deeply to the family heritage. Our ancestors lived by the simple core of respecting the earth, nurturing it, and giving back. Olive groves, sun-kissed fields, and rich gardens surrounded this very connection to our roots, and so we thought to found Bella Verde—a way to celebrate the flavors of our ancestry while honoring the very earth that nurtures us. At Bella Verde, this very soul of respect for the earth tells the story of each plate of food that comes out, merging the old with modern sustainable practices. The values that have been passed through generations are still followed and provide sustenance to both people and the planet with every meal. Everything from farm to table pays homage to the wisdom of our ancestors and that of the land that raised us.
